About Anatolia Kumpir

Welcome to Anatolia Kumpir, where we bring the authentic flavors of Turkish street food straight to you! We specialize in delicious, generously filled kumpir (baked potatoes), traditional lahmacun, and savory döner. Our ingredients are always fresh, and our recipes are rooted in genuine Turkish culinary traditions. Come and taste the difference!

At Anatolia Kumpir, we bring you the heart of Turkish street food with our signature Kumpir (baked potatoes), savory Döner, and delicious Lahmacun (Turkish pizza).
Our commitment is to quality, authenticity, and the rich culinary traditions of Anatolia.

We pride ourselves on using fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes. The integrity of our food and the strength of our flavors are what make us a favorite.
